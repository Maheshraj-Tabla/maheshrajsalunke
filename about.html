About Maheshraj Salunke

Maheshraj Salunke is a distinguished Tabla and Dhama Jori artist, recognized as the only musician to perform both instruments simultaneously in Solo and Accompaniment settings. His exceptional command over rhythm and deep-rooted training under the guidance of Pt.Shankar Kumbhar (Panjab Gharana), Pt. Umeshji Moghe (Delhi Gharana) and Pt. Nayan Ghosh (Farukhabad Gharana) have shaped him into a versatile and sought-after percussionist in the Indian classical music fraternity. Maheshraj Salunke is a dedicated and versatile musician actively engaged in the Indian classical music circuit. With a deep-rooted commitment to classical music and an extensive performance history, Maheshraj continues to contribute to the propagation and enrichment of India’s rich musical heritage.
